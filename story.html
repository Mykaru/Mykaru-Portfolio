Get to know me

My Story

Where it Began

My journey into the realm of 3D and digital art began with a simple curiosity and a desire to bring my Minecraft creations to life through animation, leading me to discover Mine-imator. As I delved deeper into the world of digital artistry, I stumbled upon Blender, where I found myself captivated by its endless possibilities and intuitive workflow. From crafting simple Minecraft animations to exploring the vast landscapes of Blender's capabilities, I quickly fell in love with the software, igniting a passion for 3D art that continues to inspire and drive me forward in my creative journey.

Moving Forward

In my ongoing pursuit of artistic growth and innovation, I've continued to hone my skills in Blender while simultaneously exploring new creative avenues. Immersing myself in fields like illustration and programming has broadened my horizons, allowing me to approach my projects from diverse perspectives. Additionally, I've delved into the realm of game development, investing time and effort in mastering multiple game engines such as Godot, Unreal Engine, and Unity.

As a technical artist, my passion lies in bridging the gap between the realms of art and programming/engineering. Leveraging my expertise, I strive to create immersive experiences that seamlessly blend creativity with technical prowess, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in interactive media and visual storytelling.
